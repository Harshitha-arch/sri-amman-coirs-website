# Sri Amman Coirs Website

A professional website for Sri Amman Coirs - Quality coir products since 2008.

## About the Business

Sri Amman Coirs has been manufacturing and supplying premium coir products for over 16 years. Located in Tamil Nadu, we specialize in eco-friendly, high-quality coir products.

## Products

1. **Coco Peat** - Available in 5kg and 450g packs
2. **Coco Brooms** - Various sizes (All Mix, 36 Below, 36 Above, 42 Above, 52 Above)
   - Brands: Amman Brooms, Hifi Brooms
   - 50kg packaging available
3. **Grass Broom** - Traditional natural grass fiber brooms
4. **2 Ply Yarn** - Hard twist (construction/agriculture) and Loose twist (export to Kerala)
