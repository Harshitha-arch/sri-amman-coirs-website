Who we are

Process-led coco peat manufacturing, built for repeatability

Sri Amman Coirs has processed coconut husk into coco peat and coir products since 2008. We combine dependable raw material sourcing with controlled washing, drying, and screening to deliver stable specifications for growers and substrate blenders.

Specification discipline

Logistics-ready
