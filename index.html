About Sri Amman Coirs

Your trusted partner in sustainable coconut fiber solutions

Our Story

Since 2008, Sri Amman Coirs has been at the forefront of premium coconut fiber manufacturing in Tamil Nadu. With over 17 years of expertise, we transform natural coconut husks into superior coir products that serve industries worldwide while maintaining our commitment to sustainability and excellence.

Our Commitment

Every product we craft reflects our dedication to environmental responsibility and uncompromising quality. From coco peat and handwoven brooms to specialized yarn, each item undergoes rigorous quality control to ensure it meets international standards and exceeds customer expectations.
